Which Kashmir do you want to see?

The single most important decision on a Kashmir trip is when you go. The month changes everything.

MonthWhat you'll seeNotes

Sample 5N/6D Kashmir itinerary

01Srinagar

Arrive Srinagar · Shikara + Houseboat

Arrive SXR. Transfer to houseboat on Dal Lake or Nigeen Lake. Afternoon shikara ride to Char Chinar and floating vegetable market. Overnight houseboat.

02Gulmarg

Day trip / stay Gulmarg · Gondola

Drive up to Gulmarg (2 hours). Gondola Phase 1 (Kongdoori) or Phase 2 (Apharwat Peak, 3,979m). Snow activities in winter, meadow walks in summer.

03Pahalgam

Srinagar → Pahalgam via saffron fields

Drive to Pahalgam (3 hours). Pampore saffron fields stop, Awantipora ruins, Anantnag apple orchards. Check in Pahalgam. Free evening.

04Pahalgam

Aru + Betaab + Chandanwari

Aru Valley pony/jeep, Betaab Valley (yes, that Bollywood film), Chandanwari — the Amarnath Yatra base camp.

05Sonmarg

Pahalgam → Sonmarg → Srinagar

Drive to Sonmarg (open Apr-Oct). Thajiwas Glacier pony ride. Return to Srinagar. Mughal Gardens if time permits: Nishat, Shalimar, Chashme Shahi.

06Depart

Depart Srinagar

Old city Srinagar quick tour (Jamia Masjid, Hazratbal Shrine, Shankaracharya). Transfer to airport.

Kashmir tips from Qutbi consultants

  1. Book gondola tickets in advance via the JKCCC website — queues at counter can be 3+ hours in peak.
  2. Pahalgam local taxi is a union monopoly — the transfer taxi cannot take you to Aru/Betaab. Budget for this.
  3. Winter clothing — Nov to March needs proper snow jackets. We rent them for the trip if you don't own any.
  4. Avoid Amarnath Yatra dates for pure holiday — Pahalgam gets crowded.
  5. Book 45+ days in advance for May-June peak.
  6. Dodhpathri / Yousmarg are lesser-known alternatives to overcrowded Gulmarg — ask us.
  7. Muslim travellers — Kashmir is Muslim-majority; halal food is default and prayer spaces are everywhere.

FAQs

Is Kashmir safe for tourists?
Yes — tourism operates normally in Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg. We monitor advisories daily and inform clients of any changes.
Best month for snow in Kashmir?
Late December to mid-February for Gulmarg. October-November for first snow lovers. March for winter still visible with pleasant days.
Houseboat or hotel in Srinagar?
At least one night on a houseboat — it is the Kashmir memory people talk about. Combine with hotel nights for hot showers and modern amenities.
Can seniors do Kashmir?
Yes — everything is doable at road level. Pony rides optional. We arrange oxygen support at Gulmarg for pre-existing conditions.
Kashmir + Ladakh combo?
Yes — 10N/11D combined tour via Zoji La (open June-October). We handle inner-line permits and altitude briefing.
