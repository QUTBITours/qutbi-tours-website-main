Rajasthan · Royal Heritage

Rajasthan — palaces, forts, deserts and one honest itinerary. 6N/7D from ₹24,900.

Jaipur's Amber Fort. Udaipur's City Palace on Lake Pichola. Jodhpur's blue city under Mehrangarh. Jaisalmer's golden fort and Sam desert camps. Pushkar's holy lake. Ranthambore's tigers. India's most photogenic road trip, done properly.

Best time to visit Rajasthan

  • Oct – Mar: Peak season. Cool days, cold desert nights. Perfect.
  • Apr – Jun: Hot (40°C+). Only for palace hotel stays with pools.
  • Jul – Sep: Monsoon — Udaipur beautiful, some routes waterlogged.

Sample 6N/7D Rajasthan itinerary

01Jaipur

Arrive Jaipur · Pink City

Arrive JAI. Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, City Palace, Johari & Bapu Bazaar. Evening light show at Amber.

02Jaipur

Amber Fort + Jal Mahal + Nahargarh

Elephant/jeep up to Amber Fort, Sheesh Mahal, Jal Mahal photo stop, Nahargarh Fort sunset over the Pink City.

03Pushkar

Jaipur → Pushkar → Jodhpur

Drive to Pushkar. Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake, ghats. Continue to Jodhpur (via Ajmer). Evening free.

04Jodhpur

Mehrangarh Fort + Blue City walk

Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Sardar Market at Clock Tower, blue-house lanes photo walk.

05Jaisalmer

Jodhpur → Jaisalmer · Golden Fort

Drive to Jaisalmer (5-6 hours). Living Golden Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, Salim Singh ki Haveli.

06Sam

Sam desert camp · camel safari + cultural evening

Drive to Sam Sand Dunes. Sunset camel/jeep safari, cultural evening with Rajasthani folk music, kalbeliya dance, Rajasthani thali dinner, overnight in Swiss tents.

07Depart

Fly out Jaisalmer / Jodhpur / Udaipur

Return to Jaisalmer, transfer to airport. (Or extend to Udaipur / Ranthambore.)

FAQs

Best month for Rajasthan?
November-February. October and March are also excellent shoulder months with better prices.
Palace hotels — worth the splurge?
Once in a lifetime, absolutely. Umaid Bhavan (Jodhpur), Rambagh Palace (Jaipur), Udaivilas (Udaipur) are among the world's top hotels.
Can we cover all major cities in one trip?
10-12 days for Jaipur+Udaipur+Jodhpur+Jaisalmer+Pushkar+Ranthambore. 6-7 days for the highlights.
Vegetarian and Jain food?
Rajasthan is a strong vegetarian and Jain state. Dal-baati-churma, gatte ki sabzi and ker sangri are must-try. Pure Jain menus available at almost every hotel.
