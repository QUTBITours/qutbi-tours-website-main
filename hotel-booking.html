Why book hotels through Qutbi instead of Booking.com or Agoda?

OTAs are excellent for spot-checking availability, but they are not always the cheapest place to actually book, and they cannot handle real human requests. Here is what we do that Booking.com and Agoda cannot:

  1. Wholesale contracted rates. We book through global bed-banks and DMCs at rates negotiated below OTA public prices.
  2. Human add-ons. Honeymoon cake, floral bed decor, anniversary champagne, early check-in, late check-out, connecting rooms, adjoining rooms, rooms on the same floor, high-floor rooms, sea-view guarantees, no-smoking rooms — arranged by a human, in writing, with the hotel.
  3. Dietary notes. Halal, Jain, vegetarian, allergy-safe meals — noted with the hotel's F&B manager, not sitting in a database somewhere.
  4. Family & group logistics. Booking 6 rooms across 3 nights, coordinated with pickup and city tax? Try that on an OTA.
  5. Flexible cancellation. Because we book on our credentials, we can often move dates or cancel with less friction than a customer-side OTA cancellation would allow.
  6. GST invoices. Every booking — corporate-ready.

Tips to save on hotels

  1. Ask for our contracted rate first. We're often below OTAs.
  2. Consider off-season. Kashmir in April vs. May can be ₹3,000/night cheaper.
  3. Book longer stays. Weekly rates are often 20% below nightly totals.
  4. Book direct chain properties. Marriott, Hilton, IHG loyalty tiers give room upgrades free.
  5. Ask about half-board / all-inclusive. In Maldives and Mauritius, all-inclusive is almost always cheaper than paying for meals separately.
  6. Book flexible rates for Europe. Non-refundable is 10-15% cheaper but risky for visa cases.
