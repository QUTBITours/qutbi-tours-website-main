Why book flights through Qutbi instead of an app?

You can book a flight yourself on any OTA in five minutes. So why do 25,000+ travellers use us instead?

  1. Smart origin routing. From Nashik, we compare fares out of BOM, PNQ, IXU and AMD — and factor in the surface transfer cost, time, and fatigue. A family of four flying out of Pune instead of Mumbai often saves ₹8,000–₹15,000 with only a 90-minute drive difference.
  2. Multi-city intelligence. Round-the-world, open-jaw, and stopover bookings are almost always cheaper than two separate tickets — but apps rarely price them optimally. We do.
  3. Baggage & seat maps. LCCs advertise a ₹4,900 fare that becomes ₹9,000 after 25 kg baggage + seat + meal. We show you the true fare upfront.
  4. Group fares. 10+ passengers get contracted group fares that public OTAs don't display.
  5. Change & cancel humans. When a schedule changes, you get a human, not a chatbot.
  6. GST invoices. Every booking, corporate-ready.

Flight types we handle

Domestic flights

Every Indian carrier — IndiGo, Air India, Vistara/AI, Akasa, SpiceJet — across every domestic sector. Advance-purchase, corporate contracted, senior citizen and student fares where applicable. We also process railway-to-air conversions when trains run late.

International flights

Full-service carriers (Emirates, Etihad, Qatar, Singapore Airlines, Lufthansa, British Airways, Air France, KLM, Turkish, Air India, Vistara-AI, Cathay, ANA) and LCCs (Air Arabia, flydubai, IndiGo international, Scoot, AirAsia, Wizz Air). One-way, round-trip, multi-city, open-jaw and stopover routes.

Business & first class

Contracted business-class fares are often 15-30% cheaper through us than OTA public prices. Emirates Business, Qatar QSuite, Singapore Airlines Business, Etihad Business, Turkish Business — mileage-friendly bookings, chauffeur services included where the fare permits.

Group fares

10+ passengers on the same sector get group fare contracts — locked-in price, deferred payment, name changes allowed close to travel. Ideal for weddings, extended family trips, corporate offsites and student groups.

Multi-city & open-jaw

Fly into Rome, out of Paris. Fly into Bangkok, out of Singapore. Open-jaw and multi-city itineraries save time and often money. Our air desk builds them correctly on the GDS.

Stopovers

Turkish Airlines offers Istanbul stopover hotel free of cost. Emirates offers Dubai Connect. Qatar Airways offers Doha stopover packages. Singapore Airlines has Singapore Stopover Holiday. We build these into your ticket at no extra fare cost.

Tips to save on flights

  1. Fly Tuesday/Wednesday — mid-week departures are cheapest.
  2. Book 60-75 days before — the sweet spot for international.
  3. Consider stopovers — a Dubai or Doha stopover can be free hotel + shorter first leg.
  4. Fly out of PNQ or AMD — often cheaper than BOM for Gulf.
  5. Book RTW multi-city — 5-6 cities on one ticket cheaper than separate.
  6. Watch airline sales — Emirates, Qatar and Turkish run March/September flash sales.
