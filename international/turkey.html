Why Turkey is the trip of this decade for Indian travellers

Turkey used to be a niche destination. Then Cappadocia's balloon photos hit Instagram, the lira became weak, Turkish Airlines expanded connections from India, and the e-visa dropped to under 72 hours. Today, Turkey delivers the historical weight of Europe (Byzantine, Ottoman, Roman ruins), the food quality of the Mediterranean, and prices that are 40-60% lower than Schengen equivalents.

Sample 7N/8D Turkey itinerary

01Istanbul

Arrive Istanbul · Bosphorus dinner cruise

Arrive IST, private transfer to your hotel in Sultanahmet or Taksim. Evening Bosphorus dinner cruise with Turkish folklore show.

02Istanbul

Old City: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Topkapi, Grand Bazaar

Full-day guided walk of Sultanahmet: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, Basilica Cistern, ending at the Grand Bazaar for tea and shopping.

03Cappadocia

Fly to Cappadocia · Underground city + valley tour

Morning flight IST-ASR/NAV. Kaymakli underground city, Uchisar Castle viewpoint, Pigeon Valley, Goreme Open Air Museum. Evening cave-hotel check-in.

04Cappadocia

Sunrise hot-air balloon + Red / Rose Valley

4:30 AM pickup for hot-air balloon (weather permitting). Post-flight celebration with certificate. Afternoon Red & Rose Valley hike or Devrent Valley + Avanos pottery.

05Pamukkale

Cappadocia → Pamukkale (via road, or via Antalya flight)

Overnight coach or morning flight to Denizli, then to Pamukkale. Cotton Castle travertine terraces, Hierapolis ancient ruins, Cleopatra's Pool.

06Antalya

Drive to Antalya · Old town + Konyaalti beach

Scenic drive through the Taurus mountains to Antalya. Kaleici (old town) walk, Hidirlik Tower, sunset at Konyaalti Beach.

07Antalya

Duden waterfalls + boat cruise

Upper & Lower Duden waterfalls, boat cruise along Antalya coast to see caves and cliffs. Evening free for old town dining.

08Depart

Antalya → Istanbul → home

Domestic flight AYT-IST connecting to international home.

Turkey e-visa

Indian passport holders eligible for e-visa if they hold a valid US, UK, Schengen, Australian or Irish visa (or a residence permit from these countries), OR through a paper visa application. Processing 24-72 hours. Fee ~USD 43. We handle it.

Halal-friendly + culturally rich

Turkey is a Muslim-majority country with fully halal food across restaurants. Vegetarian and Jain preferences are also easy: meze culture, pide, gozleme, imam bayildi, cheese-based mains, and excellent Indian restaurants in Istanbul (Zafferano, Namaste, Musafir).

FAQs

Do balloons fly every day in Cappadocia?
On average 250-280 days a year, weather-permitting. If cancelled for weather, we refund the balloon fee. We recommend planning 3 nights in Cappadocia for two attempts.
Is Turkey safe right now?
Tourist areas (Istanbul, Cappadocia, Antalya, Pamukkale) are safe with standard precautions. We monitor advisories daily.
How much is the balloon ride?
Standard shared balloon ~USD 200-250 per person (included in our packages). Deluxe balloons (fewer passengers, longer flight) higher.
Turkey visa or Turkey e-visa?
If you hold a valid US/UK/Schengen visa or residence permit, e-visa in 24-72 hours. Otherwise paper visa at Turkish consulate — 10-15 working days.
Cave hotel worth it?
Absolutely — Cappadocia's cave hotels (Sultan Cave Suites, Mithra Cave Hotel, Museum Hotel) are once-in-a-lifetime.
