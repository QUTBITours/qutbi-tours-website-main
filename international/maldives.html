The Maldives most agents don't tell you about

Maldives is 1,192 islands. Each resort is its own private island. Which island you pick determines every hour of your trip — the reef quality, the transfer time, the food style, the beach shape, the sunset direction. An honest Maldives quote has less to do with the hotel star rating and more to do with three questions:

  1. Speedboat or seaplane transfer? Speedboat islands are 15-45 min from Malé (MLE) — cheaper, faster, but often more crowded. Seaplane islands are 30-45 min flight — spectacular arrival, more remote, but seaplanes don't fly at night.
  2. Which meal plan? BB (bed & breakfast), HB (half board — breakfast + dinner), FB (full board), AI (all-inclusive — meals + drinks + activities). On a resort island where there is nowhere else to eat, AI is almost always the cheapest total spend.
  3. Water villa or beach villa? Water villas are the Insta-shot but come at 40-70% premium. Beach villas often have direct beach and lagoon access with quicker snorkelling.

Our Maldives desk lays out these three choices honestly for your budget — before you commit.

Maldives visa

Indian passport holders get a free 30-day visa on arrival. Required: valid passport (6+ months), confirmed return ticket, resort booking confirmation. No fee.

Beyond honeymoons — Maldives for families

Family-friendly resorts (Kurumba, Kandima, Kandolhu, Kuredu, Sun Siyam) offer kids' clubs, family villas with 2-3 bedrooms, watersports, dolphin cruises and shallow-lagoon snorkelling perfect for children. We can also arrange Male-city + local island (Maafushi) budget stays that make Maldives accessible from ₹35,000 per person for adventurous, non-resort travellers.

Local island stays — budget Maldives

For adventurous travellers, guesthouses on local islands (Maafushi, Guraidhoo, Ukulhas, Thoddoo, Dhigurah) offer:

  • Budget stays from ₹4,500-9,000/night
  • Snorkelling, sandbank trips, whale-shark tours
  • Home-cooked Maldivian meals
  • Local-culture immersion

Note: local islands are conservative Muslim communities — dress modestly, no alcohol, "Bikini Beach" areas are designated. Great for backpackers and cultural travellers.

FAQs

What is the cheapest way to visit Maldives?
Local island stays at Maafushi or Ukulhas — from ₹35,000-55,000 per person including flights, 3-4 nights and excursions.
Water villa or beach villa for honeymoon?
Water villa for the classic experience. Beach villa if you want direct swim access and a house-reef property. Best of both: 2 nights beach + 2 nights water villa.
Is Maldives good for families with kids?
Yes — many resorts have excellent kids' clubs and family villas. We recommend speedboat-transfer islands for shorter travel time with young children.
Are seaplane transfers safe?
Yes — extremely well-run by Trans Maldivian Airways and Manta Air. They operate only in daylight (roughly 6 AM–4 PM), so flight-timing coordination matters.
Can I find Indian food in Maldives?
Most 4-5★ resorts have Indian cuisine on the menu or in an in-house Indian restaurant. Vegetarian and Jain preferences are handled easily if pre-noted.
Is All-Inclusive worth it?
Almost always yes for stays of 3+ nights, because on a private-island resort there is nowhere else to eat and F&B prices are steep.
