Why Dubai is the world's most-booked short international holiday

Dubai is where every Nashik family goes for its first international trip — and there is a reason. Four-hour flight from Mumbai. Visa in 3-4 days. Everyone speaks English, Hindi and Urdu. Halal food everywhere, vegetarian and Jain restaurants in every mall. Cash and card are frictionless. The city is fastidiously safe, even at 2 a.m. It is, without exaggeration, the easiest possible international introduction for an Indian traveller.

Dubai visa — what you need to know

UAE tourist visa is fully e-visa — no consulate visit, no biometrics for standard applications. Processing time 3-4 working days; express service 24-48 working hours available.

  • Passport scan (colour, minimum 6 months validity)
  • Recent passport-size photograph (colour, white background)
  • Confirmed return ticket
  • Hotel booking
  • Applicable service fee

FAQs

How much does a Dubai trip cost from Nashik?
Our 5N/6D package starts from ₹42,900 per person on twin sharing in a 3-star hotel with visa, flights, hotel, breakfast, transfers and 4 tours. 4-star from ₹54,900, 5-star from ₹78,900.
Do I need a visa for Dubai?
Yes — a UAE tourist e-visa. We process it in 3-4 working days (express 24-48 hours available).
Which is the best time to visit Dubai?
November to March for pleasant weather. June-September for the deepest hotel discounts and DSS shopping season, but limit outdoor activities to sunrise/sunset.
Is Dubai safe for solo female travellers?
Yes — extremely safe, day or night. Standard travel precautions apply.
Can I find pure vegetarian and Jain food?
Very easily. Multiple Indian restaurants across Deira, Bur Dubai, Karama and the malls serve pure veg and Jain menus.
Can I fly from Nashik directly?
Nashik does not have direct international flights. Best options: Mumbai (BOM) 3.5 hours, Pune (PNQ) 3 hours (often cheaper). Airport transfer from Nashik included on request.
Can we combine Dubai with Abu Dhabi?
Yes — most our packages include a full-day Abu Dhabi tour. Multi-emirate extensions to Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah are also available.
Is Burj Khalifa 148th floor worth the upgrade?
If you can afford it, yes — the 148th "At the Top SKY" lounge is markedly less crowded and includes refreshments. Otherwise 124th is excellent value.
Are children's fares available?
Yes — child rates with bed and without bed (2-12 years). Infant fares (under 2) charged separately by airline.
What about honeymoon inclusions?
Floral bed decor, cake, dinner arrangements, in-room breakfast — we coordinate with the hotel in writing before check-in.
