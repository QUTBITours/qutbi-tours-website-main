Singapore · Family Favourite

Singapore — the world's most family-friendly first-international-trip. 4N/5D from ₹49,900.

Universal Studios. Sentosa. Gardens by the Bay. Night Safari. Marina Bay Sands. Cable car. Merlion selfie. Chilli crab dinner. Little India for prayers. If you have never taken your family abroad, Singapore is the frictionless answer.

Why Singapore for first-time Indian families

Compact geography (whole country crossable in under an hour), MRT that never fails, universal English + Tamil signage, a strong Indian community, temples/mosques/gurudwaras in one square kilometre at Little India, spotless safety, and a theme-park density that pays off for kids age 4 to 40. Singapore is the trip that removes every excuse.

Best time to visit

Year-round. Marginally drier February-April. Great Singapore Sale June-July. Christmas illuminations December. Avoid the F1 weekend if you're not attending — the city gets locked down.

Sample 4N/5D itinerary

01Arrive

Arrive Changi · Jewel + Marina Bay Sands SkyPark

Arrive at Changi (world's best airport for a reason). Head to Jewel — Rain Vortex, canopy. Check into hotel. Evening Marina Bay Sands SkyPark, Merlion Park, Spectra light-water show.

02Sentosa

Full-day Universal Studios / Sentosa

Universal Studios all day — Battlestar Galactica, Transformers, Mummy, Jurassic Park. Evening at Adventure Cove or S.E.A. Aquarium.

03Gardens

Gardens by the Bay + Night Safari

Morning Cloud Forest and Flower Dome. Afternoon Little India for lunch and shopping. Evening Night Safari with tram + walking trails.

04City

City tour + Sentosa Cable Car + Wings of Time

Chinatown, Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, Fort Canning, Sentosa Cable Car ride, Wings of Time evening show on the beach.

05Depart

Free time · Departure

Late check-out, shopping at Orchard Road, transfer to Changi.

FAQs

Is Singapore expensive?
Attractions and meals are pricier than Bangkok/KL. Hotels are competitive with Dubai. Overall, budget ₹4,000-6,000/day per person on the ground for food + local transport + attractions.
Universal Studios or Sentosa Beach?
If you have young children under 7, split the day between Universal (morning) and Sentosa Beach (evening). Kids 8+ get more from a full Universal day.
Is vegetarian food easy?
Extremely — Little India alone has dozens of pure-veg options. Every food court has vegetarian stalls. Jain food available at multiple restaurants.
How to save on Singapore attractions?
Multi-attraction passes (Klook, Changi Recommends, Sentosa Fun Pass) save 15-25%. We include the most-visited ones in our package.
