Why Bali is India's #1 honeymoon destination

Bali offers something rare: 5-star private pool villas at prices that would get you a basic 3-star in Europe. The Balinese hospitality, deeply spiritual and quietly warm, is unlike anywhere else in Southeast Asia. Volcanic sunrises at Mount Batur, terraced rice fields at Tegallalang, waterfall meditation at Sekumpul, seafood dinners on the beach at Jimbaran — Bali packs more per rupee than almost any honeymoon destination on earth.

Sample 6N/7D Bali honeymoon itinerary

01Ubud

Arrive Denpasar · Transfer to Ubud

VOA at DPS airport, private transfer to Ubud (1.5 hours). Check in to your pool villa with floral bath decor and welcome cake. Evening walk on Ubud's Monkey Forest Road, dinner overlooking rice terraces.

02Ubud

Kintamani + Tegallalang + Bali Swing

Sunrise at Mount Batur viewpoint (optional trek), coffee plantation stop, Tegallalang rice terraces, Bali Swing on the jungle canopy, Ubud Palace and Ubud Market.

03Seminyak

Transfer to Seminyak · Beach + Sunset

Drive to Seminyak (1 hour) via Tanah Lot temple. Check in to your beachfront villa. Afternoon at Seminyak Beach, sunset at Ku De Ta or Potato Head, dinner at La Lucciola.

04Nusa Penida

Day trip · Kelingking & Angel's Billabong

Fast boat to Nusa Penida island. Kelingking Beach (T-Rex viewpoint), Broken Beach, Angel's Billabong, Crystal Bay. Long day but unmissable photography.

05Uluwatu

Uluwatu temple + Kecak dance + Jimbaran seafood dinner

Free morning at your villa or spa. Afternoon: Uluwatu Temple perched on the cliff, sunset Kecak fire dance, drive to Jimbaran Bay for candlelit seafood dinner on the sand.

06Villa day

Full day at leisure · optional couple spa

A full villa/pool day — because a honeymoon needs at least one day of nothing. Optional 90-minute traditional Balinese couple spa, complimentary in some villas.

07Depart

Departure transfer

Breakfast, private transfer to DPS.

Bali visa — Visa on Arrival

Indians get Visa on Arrival at DPS (Denpasar) for 30 days at IDR 500,000 (~₹2,700). We can also pre-issue an e-VOA to reduce airport queue time. Passport must be valid 6+ months from arrival.

Beyond honeymoons — Bali for families & friends

For families

Waterbom Bali (kids water park), Bali Safari & Marine Park, Bali Bird Park, cooking classes for teens, banana boat and parasailing at Tanjung Benoa, cultural performances at GWK Cultural Park.

For adventure

White-water rafting on Ayung River, ATV rides through rice fields, canyoning near Gitgit, sunrise volcano trek to Mount Batur, surfing lessons at Kuta or Canggu.

For culture

Tirta Empul water temple purification ritual, Tanah Lot sunset, Besakih Mother Temple, Ubud royal palace performances, traditional Balinese cooking class, batik workshops.

FAQs

What is the cheapest month to visit Bali?
February and November — end of rainy season, deep hotel discounts. April and October offer the best weather-value balance.
Do we get a private driver for the entire trip?
Yes — private car with English-speaking driver-guide across your Ubud–Seminyak–Uluwatu itinerary.
Is Bali safe for Indian honeymooners?
Very safe. Indian visitors are numerous, English is widely spoken, healthcare is good. Standard travel precautions apply.
Can we get Indian food easily?
Yes — Seminyak, Kuta and Ubud have several excellent Indian restaurants. Jain and pure-veg options are also available.
Is Nusa Penida day trip worth it?
Absolutely — it is the most photographed part of Bali. Long day (12+ hours), but unmatched scenery. Skip if the sea is rough or you get sea-sick easily.
What's a realistic honeymoon budget for Bali?
4-star: ₹1.1 lakh per couple. 5-star pool villa: ₹1.6 lakh per couple. Ultra-luxury: ₹2.9 lakh+.
