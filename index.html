Frequently asked

Answers before you ask.

Is Qutbi Tours & Holidays a registered travel agency in Nashik?
Yes — GST-registered, IATA-linked through our consolidator partners, TAFI-affiliated, with all bookings on registered GST invoices.
Do you serve travellers outside Nashik?
Yes — Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Ahmednagar, Dhule, Jalgaon, Surat, Vadodara, Ahmedabad and internationally from the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain and Kuwait.
How quickly can I get a Dubai visa?
Standard tourist visas in 3–4 working days; express service where available in 24–48 working hours, subject to UAE immigration.
Are your prices cheaper than MakeMyTrip / Yatra?
For visas, offline packages, honeymoons and complex international trips — usually yes, because we contract inventory directly. For pure economy domestic flights, OTAs sometimes match us. We always show a live comparison.
Can I book only a flight or only a hotel?
Absolutely. Single flights, single hotels, single visas, single insurance policies — happily.
Do you offer EMI or instalment plans?
Yes — credit-card EMI on HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, and no-cost EMI on select destinations. Corporate clients get credit terms.
Do you handle rejected visa cases?
Yes — rebuilt files are our specialty. We audit the previous rejection, fix documentation and financial-tie weaknesses, and often succeed where the earlier attempt failed.
What if my flight is cancelled or my hotel has a problem?
One WhatsApp to our 24×7 line and a real person starts solving it. We do this hundreds of times a year.
